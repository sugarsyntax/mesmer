What is hypnosis?

Hypnosis is a natural phenomenon that is part of our creaturehood. It is a heightened state of awareness, a gift when directed towards the right direction with the guidance of a hypnotherapist. Hypnosis is activated by an overload of message units. When we go into overload, our conscious mind gets distracted and the subconscious mind takes over, taking in random suggestions from the environment: some negative, some positive.


One example of uncontrolled environmental hypnosis occurs after a long, hard day at work. You get in the car, close the heavy door, and feel your shoulders ache. The stretch from grabbing the seatbelt only makes you feel more tired, and your back hurts. Your boss’ upsetting words keep playing over in your head like a broken record. As you turn on the engine, the news comes up on the static radio station. The announcer goes on about the bad weather, and you remember you have to do laundry. You start driving, and suddenly, you are home. You don't remember how you got there: you know you were driving, but you can’t remember doing it. By enhancing your natural hypnosis in a controlled, safe environment, you can become absolutely aware of your surroundings. As you enter a heightened state of awareness, relaxation takes over, allowing new positive beliefs in and releasing old bad habits, reprogramming yourself for success, all with care, safety, and guidance of a professional.
